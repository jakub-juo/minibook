The School of Low Probability

by Jakubko Vadovič

How to begin your quest

Are you ready for an amazing adventure — with twists and turns, exciting action and puzzles to solve? Then this book is for you!

The School of Low Probability is no ordinary book — you don't read the pages in order, 1, 2, 3... Instead you must jump to and fro, forwards and back, as the plot unfolds. Sometimes you may lose your way, but the story will soon guide you back to where you need to be.

The story begins on page 3. Very soon you will have problems to solve and choices to make. The choices will look something like this:

  • If you think the correct answer is A, go to page 1 🐢
  • If you think the correct answer is B, go to page 2 🕵️

Your task is to solve each problem and make the right choice. So, if you think the correct answer is A, you turn to page 1 and look for the 🐢 symbol. That's where you will find the next part of the story.

But what happens if you make the wrong choice? Don't worry! The text will explain where you went wrong and send you back to try again.

The problems in this adventure are all about measuring uncertainty. There's some uncertainty in everything we ever perceive, think, say and do. Hardly anything is absolutely certain, or perhaps nothing ever is. That fact itself is uncertain. The best we can do is recognize that uncertainty can be measured, and some things are way more or way less probable than others.

Welcome to the School of Low Probability

You’re walking down the hallway of your school. You're on your way to the canteen, when something strange happens. Suddenly, everything around you comes to a complete halt. The sounds of footsteps, chatter, and the usual hum of the school are gone—replaced by an eerie silence. You glance around, but it’s as if time itself has frozen.

And then, you hear it. A squeaky voice breaks the stillness. You look down and, to your surprise, see a small hamster climbing up your leg. Its tiny paws scramble for a good grip, and it pauses for a moment, looking up at you with big, curious eyes.

Without a word, the hamster opens its mouth and starts speaking.

"You’ve noticed, haven’t you? Time’s frozen, it must be the works of the Time thief! I ran out of the backpack of your classmate, his name is...". And before the hamster could finish the sentence a puff of smoke materialized in front of you.

  • If you'd like to run home, jump to 🏠
  • If you're ready to face whatever comes out of the puff of smoke, jump to 💨
🏠

You don't hesitate a second. You run out the door. Outside, you would see everything frozen in time, if only you hadn't frozen in time yourself as well. This is the END. You can restart the book.

END
